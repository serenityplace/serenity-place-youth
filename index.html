Welcome Home

A Serenity Place is YOUR space. You are safe here.

This site is just for you. Learn about your rights, who you can reach out to, house expectations, and resources for your future.

Your Rights

These belong to you. Nobody can take them away.

  • 1To be treated with respect and dignity at all times
  • 2To feel safe and not be harmed, threatened, or abused
  • 3To have your medical and mental health needs met
  • 4To attend school and get help with your education
  • 5To speak privately with your attorney, caseworker, or ombudsman
  • 6To be part of decisions about your life and your service plan
  • 7To practice your religion and culture
  • 8To file a complaint or grievance without being punished
  • 9To have contact with your family (when approved by the court)
  • 10To have your personal information kept confidential

The Ombudsman

Someone in YOUR corner who doesn't work for the group home or caseworkers.

The Ombudsman makes sure you're being treated fairly. Everything is confidential.

House Rules

These keep everyone safe. When in doubt, talk it out.

⏰ Timeliness & School

Get up and ready as requested. Lunches packed before 8PM.
Consequence: Allowance deducted and/or no electronics for a day
Attend school daily.
Consequence: No electronics that day
Sick at home? Electronics after 5PM. Suspended? No electronics until return. D = no electronics at school. F = no electronics until grade is up.

👗 Dress Code

Always dress appropriately. No revealing clothes.
Consequence: Allowance deducted and/or no electronics

🚫 Drug Free

No contraband: vapes, nicotine, drugs, lighters, charging devices.
Consequence: No electronics for 30 days

💊 Medication

Take medicine as directed, on time, in presence of staff.
Consequence: No electronics that day

🗣️ Behaviors

No profanity. Bad day? Regroup.
Consequence: No electronics that day
Bullying/threats NOT tolerated.
Consequence: No electronics 48 hours; possible removal

🔒 Safety

Never enter office/locked areas unless invited. Never enter another youth's bedroom. Doors/windows locked. Blinds closed at night.
Consequence: No electronics 48 hours

🍽️ Kitchen

No stove/oven without supervision. Clean up after meals. Eat only in kitchen/dining.

📱 Electronics

No electronics in restrooms. Elementary/Middle: 9AM-8PM. High school: 9AM-9PM. 1 device at a time.

🌙 Bedtime

Upstairs by 8PM. Phones collected at 9PM.

🧹 Chores

Done by 7PM daily.

🚗 Vehicle & Outings

Never unsupervised in car. No eating/calls in vehicles. Stay with staff on outings. Max $5/month from allowance.

💡 When in doubt, talk it out! Call Mrs. Serena or Ms. Alyssa.

File a Grievance

If you feel your rights have been denied, you have the right to file a grievance. You will NOT be punished.

What is a Grievance?

A formal complaint you can file at any time if you feel your rights have been denied or abused.

You can file at any time. The sooner you file, the easier it is to investigate.

1
Submit using the form below
2
Director investigates
3
Resolution within 20 business days
4
If not satisfied, request Board hearing
